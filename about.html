Mission and Purpose
Welcome to the "Electricity Consumption in Engineering Colleges" project! Our mission is to shed light on energy usage in engineering colleges and contribute to sustainable practices in educational institutions. We believe that understanding and optimizing electricity consumption can lead to a greener and more efficient future for these campuses.
Our Approach

To achieve our goal, we employ rigorous data collection and analysis methods. We collaborate with engineering colleges and leverage publicly available data sources to compile accurate information about electricity consumption on campuses.

key Findings
We've already made some fascinating discoveries regarding electricity consumption patterns in engineering colleges, which you can explore in our reports and articles. Our findings are intended to guide colleges in making informed decisions about energy efficiency.
Acknowlegment
We extend our heartfelt thanks to all the institutions and individuals who have supported us on this journey. Your contributions have been instrumental in advancing our research and promoting sustainable energy practices.
Privacy and Data Usage
We are committed to safeguarding your privacy. Our data collection and usage adhere to strict ethical standards and are outlined in our Privacy Policy. We respect your data and your trust in us.
